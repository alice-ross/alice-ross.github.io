About

Researcher focusing on the 'human' side of human-computer interaction. Asks a lot of questions.

Publications

  • Alice Ross, Ariadna Sanchez and Nina Markl. Beyond the binary: Limitations and possibilities of gender-related speech technology research. In IEEE Spoken Language Technology Workshop 2024, 526-532.
  • Alice Ross, Martin Corley and Catherine Lai. 2024. Is there an uncanny valley for speech? Investigating listeners’ evaluations of realistic TTS voices. In Speech Prosody 2024, 1115-1119.

Research interests

  • User experiences and evaluations of realistic synthesised speech
  • Speech technology in language acquisition and development
  • AI ethics, bias, accessibility, and digital exclusion
  • Multimodal human communication, from gesture to memes
  • Disfluency, speech and articulation disorders
